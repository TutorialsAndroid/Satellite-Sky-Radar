Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2026

Satellite Sky Radar (“the App”) is developed by Heatic Apps. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not require account registration and does not access your contacts, files, camera, microphone, or any other personal information.

  • No name, email, or phone number is collected.
  • No location data is stored or sent to any server.
  • No advertising identifiers or analytics are used.

2. How Location Data Is Used

The App requests ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION permission solely to read raw GNSS satellite information (azimuth, elevation, signal strength, etc.) from your device’s GPS receiver. This is the core functionality of the App. All satellite data remains on your device and is never transmitted off‑device.

3. Third‑Party Services

The App does not integrate any third‑party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertisement networks. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. You are advised to review this policy periodically.
